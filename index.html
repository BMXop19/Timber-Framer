Walls (branching tree)
Wall 1 is the root. Each other wall branches from any wall — at its end, start, or a distance along it (a mid-wall tee) — turning left/right/straight. Use the per-wall ↰ Left / ↱ Right / ⊥ Tee buttons to spawn branches off that wall. Corners, T- and +-junctions & closed loops are framed automatically (3-stud default).
